English Centers vs Self-Study | Which Is Better for You?

About this Episode

🎙️ Welcome to English with me! — the podcast that helps you learn real English, naturally. In this episode, Rebecca and Mike break down one of the biggest questions for learners: Should you join a center or study on your own? We talk about the pros and cons of both methods and how to stay motivated regardless of what you choose.

Vocabulary & Keywords

Word / Phrase Definition Example
Self-disciplined The ability to control oneself and make oneself work hard. "Self-study requires you to be very self-disciplined."
Immersive Environment Surrounding yourself with the language you are learning. "Going to a center provides an immersive environment."
Pros and Cons The advantages and disadvantages of something. "We weighed the pros and cons before making a decision."
Curriculum The subjects comprising a course of study in a school or college. "The center has a very structured curriculum."

Practice Questions

  • What do you think is the biggest advantage of studying at an English center?
  • How do you keep yourself motivated when studying by yourself?
  • In your opinion, is it possible to reach a high level of English through self-study only?
