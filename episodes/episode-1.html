Climate Change in English: Key Vocabulary & Real Conversations

About this Episode

Want to talk about Climate Change in English — but don't know the right words? 🌍 In this episode of English with me!, Rebecca and Mike break down everything from "greenhouse gases" to "sustainability". This conversation is designed to help you understand environmental news and participate in discussions about our planet's future.

Vocabulary & Keywords

Word / Phrase Definition Example
Sustainability The ability to be maintained at a certain rate or level. "The company is focusing on environmental sustainability."
Carbon Footprint The total amount of greenhouse gases produced by an individual. "Eating less meat can help reduce your carbon footprint."
Renewable Energy Energy from a source that is not depleted when used. "Solar and wind power are forms of renewable energy."
Greenhouse Effect The trapping of the sun's warmth in a planet's lower atmosphere. "The greenhouse effect is the main driver of global warming."
